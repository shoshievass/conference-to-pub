Conference → Publication

Where papers presented at six applied-micro/IO conferences ended up: NBER SI IO (2015–2026), NBER IO Spring program meeting (2012–2026), Cowles Structural Micro / Models & Measurement (2016–2019, 2021–2026), the Utah Winter Business Economics Conference (2010–2026), the FTC Microeconomics Conference (2008–2024, 2026), and the Northwestern Antitrust Economics Conference (paper coverage 2011–2019, 2021–2025). Conference programs, publication outcomes, and current R&R statuses were collected and verified in July 2026.

Caveats. Utah 2021 was cancelled (Covid); the Utah site's 2019 program is an accidental duplicate of 2018. NBER SI 2026 (July 23–24, 2026) had not yet met when this was compiled; the other 2026 cohorts met earlier in 2026 — recent cohorts are mostly still working papers or R&Rs by construction. Cowles Structural Microeconomics (2016–2019) and Models & Measurement (2021–2026) are combined as one series; 2020 is not represented. NBER IO Spring agendas list all authors, while Cowles and Utah list only the presenter (coauthors were recovered during lookup). FTC skipped 2025; Northwestern skipped 2020, and its 2008–2010 paper programs could not be recovered. The same paper often appears at more than one of these conferences — each appearance is its own row, resolved to the same outcome. Publication status compiled via web search in July 2026; papers for which no published version was located are grouped with working papers. Working-paper series (NBER, SSRN, CEPR) are not counted as publications; an R&R is not an acceptance.
